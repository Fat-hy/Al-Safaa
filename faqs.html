Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to commonly asked questions about Al Saffah Hajj and Umrah Trust

When are Umrah services provided?

Umrah services are currently not available but we are working on providing Umrah services in the near future

What are the office locations of Al Saffah Hajj and Trust?

Al Saffah Hajj and Trust has offices in Dar es Salaam and Zanzibar, Tanzania. The Dar es Salaam office is located at Plot No. 877, House No 09, Faya/Mazengo Street. The Zanzibar office is situated at 1/3 Shatry Building First Floor, Mbuyuni Mjini.

What services are included in the Hajj trip package?

  • Air travel to and from Makkah and Madinah.
  • Domestic transportation in Makkah and Madinah.
  • Hotels in Makkah and Madinah close to Haram mosques.
  • Food provided throughout the trip.
  • Tents in Mina and Arafat.
  • Visits to historical sites in Makkah and Medina.
  • Medical treatment provided by experienced doctors.
  • Ihram clothing for men and women.
  • Travel bags.
  • Udhi-hiya slaughter.
  • Gift of 5 liters of Zam Zam water.

When is the next Hajj trip taking place?

The next Hajj trip for 1445 A.H./2024 will begin on June 6, 2024 (departure from Dar es Salaam/Zanzibar to Jeddah) and end on June 29, 2024 (return from Madinah to Dar es Salaam/Zanzibar).

What are the flight details for the Hajj trip?

The flight details for the Hajj trip are as follows:

  • Departure: Dar es Salaam/Zanzibar to Jeddah on June 6, 2024.
  • Return: Madinah to Dar es Salaam/Zanzibar on June 29, 2024.

What are the accepted payment methods for the Hajj trip?

Al Saffah Hajj and Trust accepts payments through various methods, including bank transfers and online payments, in both USD and Tanzanian Shillings (TZS).

What medical facilities are available during the Hajj trip?

Master Doctors accompany the trip to provide medical treatment and assistance to the pilgrims throughout the journey.

Are there any sightseeing opportunities during the Hajj trip?

Yes, the Hajj trip includes visits to historical sites in Makkah and Medina, allowing pilgrims to explore and learn about the rich cultural and religious heritage of the region.

What items are provided in the travel package?

  • Ihram clothing for men and women.
  • Travel bags.
  • Slaughter of Udhi-hiya.
  • Zam Zam water gift (5 liters).

When are Umrah services provided?

Umrah services are currently not available but we are working on providing Umrah services in the near future

Can I book the Hajj trip for someone else?

Yes, you can book the Hajj trip on behalf of someone else. Ensure to provide their accurate details during the booking process.

What languages are spoken by the Al Saffah Hajj and Trust staff?

The staff members at Al Saffah Hajj and Trust are fluent in multiple languages, including English, Arabic, and Swahili, to assist pilgrims from various backgrounds.

Can I get a refund if I cancel my Hajj trip booking?

Refund policies may vary. It's essential to review the terms and conditions provided by Al Saffah Hajj and Trust regarding cancellations and refunds before making a booking.
