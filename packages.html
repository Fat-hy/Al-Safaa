Hajj Package

Hajj is the pilgrimage to Mecca that every Muslim must make at least once in their lifetime if they are able. It is the most spiritual event that a Muslim experiences, observing rituals in the most sacred places in the Islamic world. Hajj is one of the five pillars of Islam and a way of expressing devotion to Allah.

At Al Safaa, we understand the importance and significance of hajj for every Muslim. That is why we offer a comprehensive and affordable hajj package that covers all your needs and expectations. We have over 20 years of experience in organizing and facilitating hajj trips for thousands of satisfied customers. We have a team of qualified and professional staff who will guide and assist you throughout your journey. We also have a network of partners and suppliers who will ensure your comfort and safety at every stage of your hajj.

Our Package Includes

  • Vaccinations provided for the required diseases, such as meningitis, yellow fever, and COVID-19, with certificates issued.
  • Accommodation near the Harams in Mecca and Medina, in great hotels, with meals included.
  • Assistance in acquiring documents, such as visa, and health insurance, with hassle-free and fast service.
  • Tents in Mina and Arafat, with the appropriate facilities, as well as meals and refreshments.
  • Facilitates Ziara of the beloved Prophet Muhammad (p.b.u.h) in the Prophet's Mosque in Madinah
  • Guided tours to historical sites, such as the Cave of Hira, Mount Uhud, Masjid al-Qiblatayn, and Jannat al-Baqi, with transportation fees included.
  • Provision of Ihram and scarf (khanga) for women.
  • Travelling bags to store your belongings
  • Air and local transport, with round-trip flights from Dar es Salaam and Zanzibar to Jeddah, and buses to and from the airport and the holy sites, with refreshments and amenities provided.
  • Gift of 5L of Zam Zam, the holy water from the well of Zam Zam.
  • Sacrifice of an animal (qurbani) as part of the rituals of Hajj.
  • Seminar and more, with lectures on the history, significance, and etiquette of hajj, as well as tips and advice on how to make the most of your hajj experience.

Hajj by Proxy

Al Safaa understands that not everyone can physically undertake the journey of Hajj. Our Hajj by Proxy service allows you to fulfill your spiritual obligations even if you cannot be physically present.

With trusted proxy performers and regular communication, we ensure that your Hajj experience is carried out with the utmost sincerity and devotion. Al Safaa provides a seamless and reliable service for those unable to perform Hajj in person.

Our Hajj by Proxy Service Includes

  • Spiritual fulfillment in absentee, enabling individuals to fulfill their Hajj journey without physically traveling.
  • Trusted proxy performers who will perform the pilgrimage on behalf of the participant.
  • Regular communication to keep participants informed about the Hajj progress.
  • Certificates provided, acknowledging the completion of the pilgrimage on your behalf.
  • Gift of 5 liters of Zam Zam water, a sacred offering from the well of Zam Zam, with a special bottle and a certificate of authenticity.
